ThìCấu trúcVí dụ
Present SimpleS + V(s/es)She works every day.
Present ContinuousS + am/is/are + V-ingI am studying now.
Present PerfectS + have/has + V3/edThey have finished.
Present Perfect Cont.S + have/has been + V-ingHe has been waiting 2 hours.
Past SimpleS + V2/edWe visited Paris.
Past ContinuousS + was/were + V-ingShe was reading at 8pm.
Past PerfectS + had + V3/edI had left before he came.
Past Perfect Cont.S + had been + V-ingThey had been walking 3 miles.
Future SimpleS + will + VIt will rain tomorrow.
Future ContinuousS + will be + V-ingI will be working at 9am.
Future PerfectS + will have + V3/edShe will have graduated by June.
Future Perfect Cont.S + will have been + V-ingWe will have been living here 5 years.
